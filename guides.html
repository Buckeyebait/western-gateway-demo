Our Guides

Any hunting camp is only as good as its guides — and we're fortunate to have some of the very best.

Woodsmanship over gimmicks

Good country and hard work — the way it should be.

All of our guides are excellent hunters, very personable, and extremely hard workers. They'll do their very best during your entire stay to keep you safe, find the best trophies, and make your hunt as enjoyable as possible. In a world where much hunting has come down to high fences, baiting and genetically altered animals, things are different here — we rely on good country, management for mature animals, and skills that come from years of experience.
