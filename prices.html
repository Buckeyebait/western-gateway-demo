Deposit & Booking

A 50% deposit is required when the hunt is booked. The balance is due on arrival at camp.

Refund Policy

A full refund is promptly given in the event of no license draw, certified proof of a disabling illness or injury, or the death of any client holding a deposit on a booked hunt.

What's Included

Meals and lodging are each charged at $50 per day and are already included in all hunt prices above.

Not Included

Sales tax, license fees, meat processing, taxidermy, and — at your discretion — gratuities for our hard-working staff.

Wyoming sales tax is 5% of meal costs and 12% of lodging cost per client. South Dakota sales tax is 5.7% of the total hunt price.
