Welcome

A diversified hunt in the heart of the Black Hills.

Western Gateway Outfitters, LLC is located in the beautiful Black Hills of northeast Wyoming and western South Dakota. We hunt both states with the camp located in Hulett, Wyoming — both areas sit in the heart of tremendous hunting country.

We spend the spring back and forth between the two states for Merriam's turkey. September is elk and archery deer in Wyoming; October we rifle hunt elk, deer and antelope. November finds us in both states for outstanding whitetails and big mule deer. We're blessed to live in an area with such diversified game and scenery.

  • Trophy Wyoming deer, antelope & elk
  • Trophy South Dakota deer & deer-antelope combos
  • Merriam's turkey in both SD and WY
