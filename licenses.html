Wyoming

Wyoming licenses & deadlines

Wyoming licenses must be applied for — the exception is spring turkey, which can be bought over the counter. Application deadlines are January 31 for elk and May 31 for deer and antelope. The preference-point application period runs July 1 through October 31.

A $21.50 conservation stamp is required for all Wyoming hunters, in addition to your license.

South Dakota licenses
